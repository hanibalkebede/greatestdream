## 1. Pre-Season: Real 2026 Coaches & Squads

Before the season starts, the game loads every club with:

- The real 2026 head coach
- The club's tactical formation (e.g., 4-2-3-1, 4-3-3)
- Tactical philosophy (possession, counter-attack, pressing, wing play)
- The real squad updated for 2026 transfers

Each squad's tactics influence player positioning, AI movement, passing patterns, and defensive behavior. This ensures realistic team gameplay and strategy.

---

## 2. Player Creation

Players can create a footballer by entering:

- Name
- Gender
- Country
- Position (excluding goalkeeper)

The system then presents 6 lower-ranked clubs suitable for starting the career, showing:

- Club coach & formation
- Squad list
- Board expectations
- Club finances

This allows the player to make strategic choices about their starting club.

---

## 3. Agent System

Each player has a personal agent who handles:

- Contract negotiation and signing
- Salary, bonuses, and clauses
- Contract renewal
- Transfers

Players can accept, reject, or renegotiate offers. Failed negotiations may collapse deals. Agents are crucial for career progression and managing transfer opportunities.

---

## 4. Signing Ceremony

Once a club is chosen, the player experiences:

- Stadium presentation
- Photo session and press conference
- Handshake with coach
- Teammates' welcome

This creates immersion and builds player identity within the team.

---

## 5. 4-Minute Training System

Before matches, the player undergoes a 4-minute training session, each minute targeting specific skills:

- **Shooting** – finishing drills, one-touch shots, and accuracy
- **Passing & Link-Up** – short passes, through balls, and off-the-ball movement
- **Positioning & Awareness** – attacking runs, staying onside, pressing opponents
- **Practice Match Simulation** – score, assist, teamwork, and pressing defenders

The outcome determines the player's starting role, coach trust, and squad status.

---

## 6. Player Controls

During matches, the player uses:

- **Arrow keys** → movement (up, down, left, right)
- **S key** → shoot (tap for low power, hold for high)
- **Z key** → pass (tap for short, hold for through pass)
- **Z + Hold** → slide tackle
- **Z + Z** → standing tackle

Tackling includes a foul risk depending on timing, adding realism.

---

## 7. Match System: Home & Away

Matches simulate real football scenarios, including pre-game cinematic sequences:

**Home Matches:** Walk from locker room, calm tactical advice from coach

**Away Matches:** Bus ride, tunnel walk, and friendly tactical talk from coach

During gameplay, the dashboard displays:

- Player and opponent goals in real-time (goal frame)
- Scorer and assist information
- Minute of each goal

This helps players track the match and make decisions during play.

---

## 8. In-Match Emotional System

Coaches react dynamically during matches:

- **Conceding a goal:** frustration, pointing at responsible players, trust and morale decrease
- **Scoring/assisting:** boosts coach trust and morale

Player morale and AI behavior respond to these events for realism.

---

## 9. Halftime & Tactical Adjustments

At halftime, the game adapts depending on the score:

**Winning/Draw:** calm tunnel walk, tactical advice, minor formation tweaks

**Losing:** angry cutscene, coach slams tactical board, points out mistakes, raises voice, tactical changes applied, squad morale reacts

Second-half adjustments occur dynamically, including aggressive tactics if the team is still losing.

---

## 10. Dashboard & Post-Match System

The game features a dynamic dashboard:

### In-Match:
- Displays goal frames with scorers, assists, and minutes
- Color-coded to distinguish team and opponent goals

### Post-Match:
- Displays final score prominently
- Shows goal summary frame
- Plays music overlay (Victory Anthem for wins, subdued music for losses)
- Highlights Best Player of the Match with stadium color effects and slow-motion replay

This system increases immersion and player satisfaction.

---

## 11. Board Pressure & Coach Sacking

The board sets expectations: league finish, cup performance, finances, and youth development

Failing targets triggers media pressure and possible coach sacking

If sacked, a new coach may change tactics and formations, and players must regain trust

---

## 12. Transfers & Career Progression

Player performance impacts:

- Transfer interest and value
- Negotiations handled by agents
- Legendary performances increase fame and reputation
- Board and media perception influence the career path

---

## 13. Trophy & Legacy System

Winning trophies triggers legendary cutscenes:

- Stadium erupts with fireworks and crowd chants
- Slow-motion focus on the player
- Coach and teammates celebrate
- Media highlights player as "legend"
- Fame rating rises, affecting future transfers and career opportunities

### Legendary Player Meter Example:

| Achievement | Fame Points |
|---|---|
| Trophy-winning goal | +50 |
| Season top scorer | +30 |
| Match-winning performance | +10/game |
| Multiple trophies | cumulative |

## 14. Complete Career Flow

1. Intro cinematic and stadium concert
2. Player creation and selection of starting club
3. Real 2026 squads and coaches loaded
4. Agent negotiation and signing ceremony
5. 4-minute training session
6. **Matchday:** Home/Away → Pre-game cinematic → First half → In-match reactions → Halftime cutscene → Second half → Dashboard updates
7. **Post-match:** Trophy-winning goal → Legendary cutscene → Fame & legacy updates
8. Transfers & contract updates
9. Season-end summary → Prepare next season → Repeat

This Football Career Mode Game combines realistic football simulation, strategic career management, immersive match experiences, and dynamic feedback systems. It allows players to feel the progression from rookie footballer to legendary player, while integrating real 2026 squads, tactical AI, dashboards, and emotional interactions for a fully immersive experience.
